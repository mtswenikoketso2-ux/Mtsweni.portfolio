Personal Background

Bafana Koketso Mtsweni was born and raised in Maboloka Village, Brits, in the North West province. He was raised by a strong single mother alongside his three siblings. Growing up in a challenging environment taught him responsibility, resilience, and discipline from an early age.

From a young age, he balanced academics with football, which helped him develop focus, teamwork, and leadership skills. He became the first person in his family to attend university, marking a proud milestone for his family and community.

At the age of 18, he enrolled at Tshwane University of Technology to pursue a National Diploma in Information Technology. By 22, he had completed all theoretical modules and began actively searching for practical experience to complete his diploma. His persistence led to a Modern Desktop Learnership opportunity in Midrand mid 2023, marking the beginning of his professional journey in Gauteng.

He later secured an internship at Unisa Enterprise (Pty) Ltd, where he demonstrated initiative, dedication, and continuous self-improvement. His performance earned him growth into a Junior IT Support Technician role. Although his employment ended due to company financial challenges (Retrenchment), the experience strengthened his personal, technical and professional capabilities.

Through his hard work, he was able to improve his family's living conditions, move them from a shack to a better house, support his siblings, and restore dignity and stability within his household. This remains one of his greatest personal victories.

He continues to actively play football and currently serves as the manager of Maboloka Fast 11 FC. In this role, he contributes to the community by guiding young boys away from crime and negative influences, encouraging them to focus on sport and education. He believes leadership means creating opportunities for others and giving back to the community.

He continues to seek contract or permanent employment opportunities, he remains committed to gaining valuable industry experience, expanding his professional network, and strengthening the foundation of his entrepreneurial ambitions since his long-term vision is to establish a sustainable IT solutions company. That vision has already begun with the development of Rei2Tech, and this portfolio website represents its first official project.
